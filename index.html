About UPI QR

A free, privacy-first tool to generate payment QR codes for any UPI ID — instantly, in your browser.

⚡

Instant Generation

QR codes appear as you type with live preview. No waiting, no loading.

🔒

100% Private

Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is ever sent to a server.

📱

Works Everywhere

Compatible with PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM, and all UPI-enabled apps.

💾

Download & Share

Save your QR as a high-quality PNG or share payment details instantly.

How to Use

  1. 1Enter the payee's name and UPI ID (e.g. name@okaxis).
  2. 2Optionally set an amount — leave blank to let the payer choose.
  3. 3Click Generate QR or watch it appear live as you type.
  4. 4Download the PNG or share the payment details.
